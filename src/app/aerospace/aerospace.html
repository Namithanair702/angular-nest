Aerospace & Defense

Aerospace & Defense at neST Digital encompasses the delivery of high-integrity, real-time avionics software for flight control and vehicle management systems. We engineer secure communication systems that guarantee resilient and encrypted data links for airborne and ground operations. Our advanced training and mission simulators create realistic environments for pilot training and tactical planning. We develop cyber-secure defense platforms integrating robust cybersecurity measures into command, control, and battlefield networks.

Our Key Focus Areas
  • DO-178C compliant safety-critical software
  • Embedded avionics and mission computer development
  • Secure communication and encrypted data link solutions
  • UAV control systems & ground control software
  • Simulation, HIL/SIL testing & mission rehearsal systems
  • Defense electronics including radar and sensor integration
  • Lifecycle support from design to certification
